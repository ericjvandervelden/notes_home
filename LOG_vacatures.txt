What department? 
You will work in the dynamic and challenging environment of the IT organization of ING Global Digital Channels.

We operate according to a Scrum Agile development process and strongly believe in the DevOps approach to delivering high-quality mission-critical software. We work with the latest technologies, tools and frameworks, which means that our Engineers work in a dynamic environment in which they are, as part of their day to day job, also continuously researching, training and learning. If you have bright ideas, they will be valued and supported by both your pears and your management.

 

Job Description 
As a Dev Engineer you work on developing and testing high quality applications, at a bank that is known for its IT innovations. Working according to the newest methods within complex IT projects. At ING we work in a DevOps manner, where the Scrum/Agile methodology and Continuous integration/Continuous Delivery practices are very important.

You will become part of the Teams working on our main Internet-facing Wholesale Banking system, Inside Business Portal. The Portal covers the entire range of ING’s Wholesale Banking products: payments & cash management, FM, Lending, etc. The Portal is accessible via web as well as mobile and tablet devices (with native apps for iOS and Android).


Our Stack 
Within Wholesale Bank Channels we predominantly use the following technologies and frameworks: Java, Angular, Polymer, etc. But also Scala is at your disposal. All based on a Tomcat/Linux/Oracle stack on which we deliver in sprints of 2 weeks fully automated new features into production. We take Continuous Integration and Continuous Delivery seriously; it is part of our genes. We have Jira, GitLab, Jenkins, Maven and Artifactory at our fingertips. We never compromise on Security. And we love people who come up with great ideas to make our systems even more secure, reliable and resilient than they currently already are!
